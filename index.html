The activities

Pottery art

This experience will give you the opportunity to touch the clay and reshape it according to your creativity.

coffee art

Are you tired of making espresso to the point that it doesn't come right with you, then this experience is for you, where you will learn how to adjust.

ballet dance

An experience that teaches you the basics of dancing and gives you a feeling of lightness and joy.

color on Canvas

Here, art will be your means of expressing what is inside you of drawing, colors, and forming artistic paintings.

resin art

A unique experience for art and beauty lovers and resin lovers. It takes you to the world of creativity through a unique painting of art.

Doll making

A fun craft, with which you create continuous joy in the form of a doll, by choosing its details and colors and adding your own creative touch to it.

Specialty coffee

An overview of specialty coffee and the difference between it and commercial coffee, and an explanation of how to properly extract espresso.

horseback riding

Do you want to live the adventure while riding a horse in an open place and among the mountains? Join us every Friday and Saturday.

Cake industry

Color the velvet buttercream in your favorite colors and the splendor of the color flow, passing through the rose petals to the fun of arranging.

English language

We take you on a journey of learning the language and facilitate it for you to enjoy all stages of its education.

Yoga sport

An experience that takes you to another world away from the pressures and problems of life, a session full of relaxation and laughter!.

Music

In this experience, enjoy the experience of music and its strings, and you will experience the joy of playing.

Bike riding

A bike tour between the mountains and plains, then enjoy a calm and relaxing yoga session with the sunset.

Deep dive

Deep diving experience is an enjoyable journey that helps you strengthen your muscles And more adventure

boxing

This journey takes you to the world of boxing, strength, challenge, fun, competition with others, and gaining more physical fitness..
